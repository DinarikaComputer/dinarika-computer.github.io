PROFILE US

UD DINARIKA COMPUTER has been established since 1997

UD. Dinarika Computer was established in 1997 on Waturenggong Street, Denpasar, through a collaboration between two individuals, Ketut Karyawan and I Nyoman Sudiasa. Initially, they rented a building measuring 4 x 6 square meters. Over time, the company moved locations several times until settling in its current location on Nangka Selatan Street, Gg. Kakak Tua I No.5, Dangin Puri Kaja, North Denpasar District, Denpasar City, Bali, in 2018.
